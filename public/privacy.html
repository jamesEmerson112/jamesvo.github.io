Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2, 2025

Overview

This privacy policy describes how this website collects and uses information when you visit jamesvo.github.io.

Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. Google Analytics collects information such as how often users visit, what pages they visit, and what other sites they used prior to coming here.

Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to collect and analyze information about site usage and report on activities and trends. Google may also collect information regarding the use of other websites, apps, and online services.

Information Collected

When you visit this site, the following information may be collected:

  • Pages you visit and time spent on each page
  • Approximate geographic location (city and country)
  • Device type and browser information
  • Referring website (how you found this site)
  • Date and time of your visit

Use of Information

The information collected is used to:

  • Understand how visitors use the site
  • Improve site content and design
  • Analyze traffic patterns and trends

Third-Party Services

This site uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc. Google's use of cookies and information collected through Google Analytics is governed by Google's Privacy Policy, which can be found at https://policies.google.com/privacy .

How to Opt-Out

You can prevent Google Analytics from recognizing you on return visits by:

  • Installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Disabling cookies in your browser settings
  • Using privacy-focused browser extensions

Data Retention

Google Analytics data is retained for 26 months from your last interaction with this site, after which it is automatically deleted.

No Personal Information

This site does not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information (such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers). No user accounts or login systems are used.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
